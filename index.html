I am a Solution Engineer at Nokia (US) in the Cloud Network Services - IP Telephony team. I joined Nokia after graduating from the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign with an MS in Computer Science. During my Master's, I interned as a Solutions Architect at Red Hat in the Telco Tigers team. Previously, I worked as an Applications Engineer at Oracle (India), where I was part of Oracle Service Cloud's Server team. I completed my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Calicut, India, in 2018.

I am broadly interested in Systems, specifically at the intersection of Cloud Computing, Networks, Databases, and Real-Time Systems. For my Master's thesis, I collaborated with Dr. Sibin Mohan's group (SyNeRCyS Lab, UIUC) to create a framework to support the live migration of real-time applications in an IoT environment. I have also done projects in other domains, such as Data Mining, Networks, Databases, and Operating Systems.
